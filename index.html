About Us

Welcome to Poddar Retreat, an enchanting oasis nestled on the banks of a pristine river. As you step into our luxurious resort, you are greeted by the gentle melody of flowing water and the serene beauty of the surrounding natural landscape. The resort is situated amidst lush greenery, with tall swaying trees providing a comforting shade on warm days. The architecture of the resort is inspired by the local heritage, seamlessly blending modern amenities with traditional elements. The main building exudes elegance with its rustic charm and harmonizes perfectly with the picturesque surroundings. Accommodations at Poddar Retreat are designed to offer the utmost comfort and tranquility. You can choose from a variety of options, including spacious rooms, cozy cottages, and private villas. Each abode boasts panoramic views of the river, allowing you to wake up to the soothing sight of water meandering through the landscape. Immerse yourself in the beauty of nature by strolling through the resort's sprawling gardens. The carefully landscaped pathways lead you to hidden nooks and benches, inviting you to pause and bask in the peaceful atmosphere. In the evenings, gather around the riverside bonfire and share stories under a star-studded sky, creating memories that will last a lifetime. For those seeking rejuvenation, our spa and wellness center offers a range of treatments and therapies that will melt away your stress. Indulge in a relaxing massage by expert therapists or unwind in the steam room, letting the gentle steam revitalize your body and mind. Culinary delights await you at our riverside restaurant, where talented chefs create exquisite dishes using locally sourced ingredients. Whether you prefer international cuisine or traditional delicacies, our diverse menu is sure to satisfy your palate. As you savor your meal, the rhythmic sounds of the flowing river provide a soothing backdrop, enhancing your dining experience. Adventure enthusiasts will find no shortage of activities at Riverside Bliss Resort. Embark on a river cruise and witness the abundant wildlife that calls the river their home. Engage in exciting water sports such as kayaking and paddleboarding, or embark on a guided nature walk to explore the rich biodiversity of the surrounding area.
